Born in Italy on June 3rd, 1994, Edoardo Cino discovered his passion for dance and music at the age of 10.
At 18, Edoardo joined the pre-prfessional dance program "Eko Dance Project” (Turin, IT), delving deep into
the captivating artistic realm envisioned by Pompea Santoro where he mastered ballet technique and opened up
to modern and contemporary dancing. In 2013, he made his first professional experiences with
"The very secret dance society" (Turin, IT), directed by Raffaele Irace.

Edoardo has been a soloist dancer at the "Saarländisches Staatstheater" (Saarbrücken, DE) since 2015, under
the direction of the versatile choreographer Stijn Celis. His performances in Saarbrücken have showcased his
skill, featuring prominently in numerous creations by Stijn Celis and interpreting the works of renowned choreographers.

Venturing into choreography, Edoardo debuted his creative spirit in 2017 by participating in the
Saarländisches Staatstheater prduction for emerging choreographers. In 2023 he successfully presented in Mannheim
"Ephemeral Dialogue 468", a short piece created in collaboration with dance artist Micaela Romano, and later
he joined Theaterhaus Jena to make the satirical dance-theater evening "Die Hundekot-Attacke".
