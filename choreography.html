Die Hundekot-Attacke

Professional Experience

During the dance activity with Saarländisches Staatstheater, Edoardo created the four short dance pieces "Leib Warm-Kalt", "I am sitting in a room", Into the light"
and "Tanzreime zum Geniessen". In 2022 he collaborates with Saarbrücken actors to make the full-evening "Tschernobyl.Stimmen" and the year after he joins
Theaterhaus Jena to make the satirical dance-theater piece "Die Hundekot-Attacke". In 2023 he also creates, together with dance artist Micaela Romano,
the dance piece "Ephemeral Dialogue 468" which was performed in Mannheim in occasion of the "Freier Tanz im Delta XII".
