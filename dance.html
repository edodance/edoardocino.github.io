Professional Experience

At the age of 18, Edoardo moves the first steps as professional dancer in the freelance scene of Torino (IT). The meeting with the choreographer
Rffaele Irace and his group "the very secret dance society" was surely one of the most important for his professional beginning. In 2015, with the help
of his teacher and mentor Pompea Santoro, Edoardo joins the Saarländisches Staatstheater of Saarbrücken (DE), directed by the choreographer Stijn Celis.
Here he has lots of opportunity to work with many renown choreographers and to mature his artistry. In 2018 he is the leading role in "Amore", a dance
piece created by Stin Celis and inspired by the novel "L'amour" of Marguerite Duras. The year after he plays again a leading role in "Verweile doch" by Kim
Brandstrup and in "Shunkin" by Celis. With the latter Edoardo establishes a fruitful artistic connection that will lead to many other successful productions
like "Prometheus", "Sound & Vision", "Winterreise" and "The privacy of things". The artisrtic research conducted with Anna Konjetzky and Moritz Ostruschjak will
also have a strong impact on his dancing. As for the repertoire, Edoardo has the privilege to perform in many famous dance pieces like "Forgotten Land" and
"Bella Figura" by Jiri Kylián, "Cacti" by Alexander Ekman, "Selon Desir" By Andonis Foniadakis, "La Stravaganza" by Anjelin Preljočaj, "Liedgut" by Richard Siegal,
"Whiteout" by Marco Göcke and he has the wonderful opportunity to dance a leading role in "Der grüne Tisch" by Kurt Jooss.

Dance studies

Edoardo beginn with dancing at the age of 10 in his hometown Avellino (IT). Here he joins the vocational school "Arte in Movimento", directed by Antonella De Angelis
and starts learning about ballet and Horton technique. In 2008 he moves in Torino (IT) with his family and focuses his dance studies on ballet with the school of
"Teatro Nuovo Torino", directed by Daniela Chianini and with private classes on Vaganova technique with the russian teacher Oxana Kichenko. The hard work of this years
is praised in 2011 with the third place in the senior soloist category at the national dance competiton "Festival Ballet" in Massa Carrara (IT), and the year after with
the first place in the same category at the national dance competition "Je Danse" in Cumiana (IT). In those years he is invited to join the new and experimental
pre-professional dance program "Eko Dance Project" led by the incredible dance artist Pompea Santoro. Her teaching brings Edoardo's dancing on new heights and new fields
and the design of the project gives him a lot of stage experience all across the country. In 2014 he gets the honor to represent his country at the Italian-Egyptian
festival in Cairo City by performing the dance solo "Pas de Danse" by Mats Ek. The learning process never stopps for Edoardo and in fact he often participates in
workshops and summer intesives in which he explores other views on dance.
